Exciting News:

Delicious Bites Cafe is Opening at DAH University



Welcome to Delicious Bites Cafe!

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of Delicious Bites Cafe at DAH University. Our cafe is the perfect place to enjoy delicious food and beverages in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.

Key Features:
  • Varied menu with mouthwatering treats.
  • Freshly brewed coffee and a wide selection of tea.
  • Cozy seating areas for studying or relaxing.
  • Free Wi-Fi for all our guests.

Location:

Delicious Bites Cafe is located on the DAH University campus, right in the heart of the academic hub. It's easily accessible, making it a convenient spot for students, faculty, and visitors to enjoy a great meal or coffee.

Opening Hours:

Our cafe will be open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends. We look forward to serving you throughout the week.

We can't wait to welcome you to Delicious Bites Cafe and become your go-to spot for delicious food and great moments. Stay tuned for exciting promotions and events!
